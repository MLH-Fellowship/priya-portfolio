University of Washington Major Finder

This application was created with the intention of helping underclassmen at the University of Washington navigate through the different majors offered by providing an in-depth explanation of how the application process works. Our group of four decided to include a section with peer feedback to give students the opportunity to view how others have rated classes they may be interested in taking.

Analysis of Vogue Models

This project was created to find out whether Vogue prefers lighter skin color models compared to darker skin-colored models on their magazine covers. My partner and I sorted and cleaned a dataset before analyzing it for trends and creating visual graphs to display our findings.

MindMeet

This design was created with the idea of having an application available for users who are primarily interested in group therapy. Our group of five decided that we wanted our users to be among two different groups: individuals who wanted to pursue group therapy and those who were already group therapists.
